WELCOME TO OUR SIDE

Currently we are providing fresh milk extracted daily from our cows to our customers. Our milk is completely organic and no chemicals are added to our milk. Given the situation in our country these days where most food products are loaded with chemical compounds, we promise to provide you a product which is completely free of these toxic add-ons. Our cows undergo extensive medical attention to make sure they are disease free and are given the best care possible. We can also proudly say that none of our cows are given any illicit steroids or growth hormones.
